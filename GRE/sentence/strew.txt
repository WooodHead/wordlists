
1. The racoons knock over the rubbish bins in search of food, and strew the contents all over the ground. 浣熊弄翻垃圾箱找吃的，把垃圾弄得满地都是。 
2. Their custom is to strew flowers over the graves. 他们的风俗是在坟墓上撒花. 
3. Get up, or I'll strew your brains upon the grass. 起来, 否则我就把你的脑浆洒在这草地上.
