
1. I believe in seasoning food before putting it on the table. 我认为在把食物端上餐桌前应该先调味。 
2. Mix the meat with the onion, carrot, and some seasoning. 把肉和洋葱、胡萝卜及一些佐料搅拌在一起。 
3. Salt is the most common seasoning. 盐是最常用的调味品. 
1. Rub the seasoning into the meat and set aside.
