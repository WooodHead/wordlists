
1. Press the "reload" button on your web browser to refresh the site and get the most current version. 点击网页浏览器上的“刷新”按钮来刷新页面，显示最新的版本。 
2. For simplicity in the design, the refresh is usually performed from a dedicated memory. 为了简化设计, 通常由一个专用存贮器进行刷新. 
3. To retrieve the latest copy of the list, click Refresh. 若要检索此列表的最新副本, 请单击“刷新”.
