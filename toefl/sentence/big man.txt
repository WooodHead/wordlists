Anthropologists studying the evolution of human communities in various parts of the world have discovered that one common stage in the process is the emergence of what are called "big men" within a single village or a collection of villages. 人类学家在研究世界不同地区人类群体的进化时，发现在这个过程中有一个共同阶段，那就是在单个或多个村落中“大人物”的出现。
