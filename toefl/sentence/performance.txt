In Berlin, for the premiere performance outside the Soviet Union of The Battleship Potemkin, film director Sergei Eisenstein worked with Austrian composer Edmund Meisel (1874-1930) on a musical score matching sound to image. 为了《前苏联战舰波将金号》在柏林的首次公演，该片导演Sergei Eisenstein与奥地利作曲家Edmund Meisel（1874-1930）合作为电影配乐。
